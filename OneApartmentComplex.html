OneApartmentComplex

    In response to COVID-19, and there is no convenient way to control access to the community, this project was developed to allow the community to conduct e-management through links to webpages and APPs, which can not only improve the efficiency of community operations, but also enable residents to get closer to community life , It is no longer necessary to obtain new information through traditional announcements or go to the management room to handle trivial matters in person. Just use the APP to grasp the big and small things in life. During the epidemic, unnecessary contact can be minimized and the risk of community infection can be reduced.

    In order to solve community problems, we developed "OneApartmentComplex" to help the community develop bette. To expand, we have established three major modules (account management, community life, community interaction) to allow system design. There are more comprehensive functions in the account management module. In the account management module, this topic has the following functions "register & sign in, personal notification". In the community life module, this question realizes the following functions "public column management, community Regulations, feedback, package mailing, community repair, car calling, visitor management, gas form, management In the community interaction module, this topic has realized the following functions "community voting, chat room, Events and registration, planning the itinerary".
